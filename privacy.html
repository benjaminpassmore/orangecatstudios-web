Privacy Policy

Warmboard Sudoku  ·  Effective Date: July 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Orange Cat Studios ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Warmboard Sudoku mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the App. The App is designed to function without collecting personally identifiable information.

Information Collected Automatically

The following information may be collected automatically through third-party services integrated into the App:

  • Usage data and analytics (puzzle completion rates, game session duration, difficulty preferences) — used to improve the App experience
  • Device information (device type, operating system version) — used for compatibility and crash reporting
  • Advertising data — if you view advertisements within the App, our advertising partner Google AdMob may collect certain information as described in Google's Privacy Policy
  • Purchase data — if you make an in-app purchase, our payment partner RevenueCat processes transaction information as described in RevenueCat's Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Serve relevant advertisements to non-premium users
  • Process in-app purchases and restore premium status
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve game design and features
  • Diagnose technical issues and crashes

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchases) — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Expo / React Native (app framework) — expo.dev/privacy

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

5. Data Retention

Game progress, statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and are not transmitted to our servers. You may clear this data at any time by uninstalling the App or using the "Reset Stats" option in Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since we do not collect personally identifiable information directly, most data rights can be exercised by clearing app data on your device. For questions about data held by our third-party partners, please contact them directly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
